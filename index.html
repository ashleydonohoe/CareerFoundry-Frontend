Frequently Asked Questions

Why did you decide to become a web developer?

I'm a creative person with a love for technology, so I tried building websites and fell in love with the field. Being a web developer gives me the opportunity to exercise my creativity, solve challenging problems, and do work that will benefit others.

What is your design process?

First, we will work together to look at your website's purpose, target audience, and your goals for the site. We will then work together to plan the design, site map, and features you need. After the website has been designed, we will test it before delivery to ensure it meets your expectations.

Who are your clients?

I'm able to work for individuals, non-profit organizations, small businesses, or corporations. Whether you need a one-time project or ongoing developer, I can help.

How much are your services?

Since each customer has a specific need and development time varies, I offer a customized quote rather than give flat rates. Feel free to contact me via phone, email, or Twitter for your quote.

Are you available to work now?

I am currently available to take on freelance projects on a short-term or long-term basis. Check out the contact page to get in touch.
